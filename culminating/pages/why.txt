- All man-made products are derived and refined from natural resources
- Certain countries and populations are subject to exploitation for jobs obtaining rare resources
- We are dependent on fossil fuels which are non-renewable and will eventually deplete
- Natural resources also support ecosystems and the environment, not just us

It is very important that we understand the situation and act sooner rather than later. There are many other
